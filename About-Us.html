About Us

A stall in BGAIN 630 Eating House. Does dried and soup noodle items like fried wonton noodles, wonton noodles, ‘fishball’ noodles, bak chor mee with choice of meepok and meekia noodles. Previously named 630 Vegetarian. Laksa and sliced fish bee hoon contain dairy - although the latter can be prepared without on request. Open Mon-Tue 7:00am-1:00am, Thu-Sun 7:00am-1:00am.
